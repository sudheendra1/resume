Pharmcare

Pharmcare is a mobile application that provides users with information about medicines by scanning the Barcode present at the back of the tablet case. This feature enables users to access comprehensive information about the medicine, including its benefits and side effects. When the user enters their symptoms, the app shows all possible medical issues related to those symptoms along with relevant precautions and care. The app also provides first aid instructions for times when there are no clinics nearby or when in a remote place. The app uses advanced algorithms and machine learning to provide accurate and up-to-date information to the users.
